Brownie Recipe

How to make the BEST Brownies ever!

Just like how grandma used to make, I really like these brownies. They are so sweet and chocolaty and I can't wait to share this recipe with you guys! Here we go, me homemade recipe for chocolate brownies.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (optional, but highly recommended!)

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan or line it with parchment paper for easy removal.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, whisking well after each. Stir in the vanilla extract for extra magic.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring gently until just combined. (Don’t overmix, or your brownies might turn into edible bricks!)
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips if using. Because more chocolate = better brownies.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread it evenly.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with just a few moist crumbs. (Not raw batter, but not bone-dry either!)
  9. the brownies cool for at least 20 minutes before cutting them. Or just eat them warm with ice cream—no judgment here!
